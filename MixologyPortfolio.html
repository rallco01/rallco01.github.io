About me

I'm a second-year uni student, enrolled at the University of Wollongong. I'm studying for my bachelor in computer science with a double major. But that's not what this portfolio is demonstrating. While studying, I have been practising Mixology, and have built up quite the portfolio (as you're about to see).

What makes this portfolio so impressive, is that I fall under the stereotypical uni student description: I'm broke (not necessarily something you want to advertise on a portfolio, but I promise it's an important detail). This means that everything I make has to be made from either cheaper or homemade ingredients. This, of course, has some issues, for example, the quality of inputs. However, I personally see to it that every cocktail on this list is made with such skill and perfection, that if made following these recipes with the right techniques, that even with the worst ingredients, you will have a fantastic cocktail.

So that's what makes this portfolio impressive. It isn't a showcase of the most expensive, top shelve liqueurs. Its a demonstration on how I use my skill, passion, and dedication to take any quality of ingredient and guarantee a top-quality result. It shows how the making of a high-quality product is not a result of the inputs, but rather a result of the knowledge, passion, and willingness to make an effort.
